About

Hey! My name is Luc (short for Luciano) and I’ve been making dumb interactive stuff since I first downloaded the Flash MX trial from albinoblacksheep.com.

I’ve graduated from York University and Sheridan College with a Bachelor of Design Honours in 2017 with a special focus on interaction and motion design. Since then I’ve been a marketing designer at Connected, and motion designer at Rogers Media.

I love creating and animating playful experiences, experimenting with new technology, learning new tools and skills, and teaching. I’d love to be designing for spatial computing, so much so that I started a club at my previous job!

I'm always interested in meeting cool people and am currently seeking new opportunities. Let's chat!
